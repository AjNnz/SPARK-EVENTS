WEDDING EVENTS

CRAFTING MOMENTS. CELEBRATING LOVE

At Spark Events, your love story is our inspiration. We believe your wedding day should be a true reflection of your relationship—unique, heartfelt, and unforgettable. Whether you're dreaming of an intimate garden ceremony, a beachfront celebration, or a grand ballroom reception, we’re here to turn your vision into reality with grace, creativity, and seamless coordination.

From day one, we guide you through every step—venue selection, theme creation, vendor management, timelines, and guest experiences. We take pride in reducing your stress while amplifying the joy, ensuring every detail is carefully curated and every moment feels magical. Your day should be filled with love, not logistics—and that's where we come in.

Our team specializes in design and storytelling through personalized florals, elegant table settings, mood lighting, and bespoke installations that reflect your personality as a couple. Whether you desire timeless elegance or modern flair, we bring it to life with a sparkle that’s all your own.

Our Packages

We offer a range of wedding packages to suit your unique love story, from intimate ceremonies to grand celebrations. Each package is designed to make your special day unforgettable.

✦ Romance Spark

Perfect for intimate weddings or elopements. Includes:

  • Ceremony planning and basic coordination
  • Venue sourcing assistance
  • Floral bouquets and boutonnières
  • Minimal décor and backdrop
  • On-the-day coordination

✦ Golden Spark

Ideal for mid-size weddings with full service needs. Includes:

  • Full wedding planning and scheduling
  • Theme and design consultation
  • Vendor booking and communication
  • Reception styling and guest management
  • Ceremony and reception coordination

✦ Diamond Spark

For luxurious, large-scale dream weddings. Includes:

  • Complete planning and execution
  • Custom floral design and grand venue styling
  • Entertainment booking and special performances
  • Invitation and stationery design
  • Full team of coordinators and day-of management
